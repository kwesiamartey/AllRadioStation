Privacy Policy for Radio Delight

LORNAMOBILEAPPSDEV provides users with access to a variety of radio stations. We are dedicated to protecting the privacy and security of our users' information. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage the information collected by the Radio Delight app.

Information Collection

Personal Information: Radio Delight does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require users to create accounts or provide details like names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Usage Data: Radio Delight does not collect any usage data that can be linked to individual users. We do not track users' activities within the app.

Information Usage

Data Storage: Radio Delight does not store any user data on our servers. All data remains on the user's device and is not shared with any external servers or third parties.

Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics tools or services to gather information about app usage.

Information Sharing

Third-Party Services: Radio Delight does not share any user information with third-party services or advertisers.

Data Security

Security Measures: While we take reasonable steps to secure user data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Age Limitation: Radio Delight is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates: We reserve the right to update or modify our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at lornamobileappsdev@gmail.com.
