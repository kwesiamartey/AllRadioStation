Privacy Policy for Radio Delight

Radio Delight is a mobile application developed by LORNAMOBILEAPPSDEV for providing users with access to various radio stations. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users' information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information collected by the Radio Delight app.

Information Collection:

Personal Information: Radio Delight does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require users to create accounts or provide any personal details such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Usage Data: Radio Delight does not collect any usage data that can be used to identify individual users. We do not track users' activities within the app.

Information Usage:

Data Storage: Radio Delight does not store any user data on our servers. All data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics tools or services to collect information about app usage.

Information Sharing:

Third-Party Services: Radio Delight does not share any user information with third-party services or advertisers.

Data Security:

Security Measures: While we take reasonable measures to secure user data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy:

Age Limitation: Radio Delight is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

Updates: We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be reflected within the app and on this page.

Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at lornamobileappsdev@gmail.com. This Privacy Policy was last updated on 1/01/2024.
